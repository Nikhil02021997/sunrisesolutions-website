Terms & Conditions for Recruitment Assignments

Placement Charges

Our professional fees for the selection of a candidate will be 8.33% of the annual Cost to Company (CTC) offered to the candidate.

Service Tax / GST

GST will be charged at 18% on the total billing amount, as applicable and as per Government notifications.

Travel Expenses

Any travel expenses incurred by outstation candidates called for interviews shall be reimbursed directly by the client to the candidate, as per prevailing market practices.

Correspondence with Candidates

Copies of all communication with candidates including interview call letters, appointment letters, and offers should be marked to Sunrise Solutions to ensure proper follow-up and candidate commitment.

Placement Credit

Candidates referred by Sunrise Solutions and selected by the client will be deemed as our placement, even if selected for a different role. This clause is valid for a period of one year from the date of submission of the candidate’s resume.

Replacement Guarantee

If a candidate appointed through Sunrise Solutions leaves the organization on their own within 90 days of joining, we commit to replacing the candidate at no additional cost.

Payment Terms

All invoices raised by Sunrise Solutions must be settled within 30 days from the date of joining of the candidate. Payments should be made via account payee cheque or bank transfer in favour of “Sunrise Solutions.”
