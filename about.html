About Sunrise Solutions

A trusted recruitment and HR consulting partner helping organizations build strong, sustainable teams through ethical and consultative hiring.

Who We Are

Sunrise Solutions is a professionally managed recruitment and HR consultancy based in Faridabad, Haryana. We are driven by a clear objective — to deliver ethical, reliable, and result-oriented hiring solutions for organizations across diverse industries.

With strong industry knowledge and seasoned leadership expertise, we work as a strategic hiring partner rather than just a recruitment service provider. Our approach focuses on understanding business objectives, organizational culture, and long-term workforce requirements.

We believe recruitment is not merely about filling vacancies. It is about identifying the right talent, aligning skills with business needs, and building teams that contribute to sustainable growth and long-term success.

What We Do

Sunrise Solutions provides end-to-end recruitment and talent acquisition support — from requirement analysis and candidate sourcing to screening, coordination, and onboarding assistance.

We support organizations across technical and non-technical roles, helping them address challenges such as time-sensitive hiring needs, evolving skill requirements, and maintaining quality at scale.

Our consultative hiring model ensures every recruitment decision is structured, data-informed, and aligned with long-term organizational goals, enabling our clients to focus on growth while we take ownership of their hiring needs.
