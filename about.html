About Sunrise Solutions

Sunrise Solutions is a professionally managed recruitment and HR consultancy based in Faridabad, Haryana. Established in July 2024, we specialize in delivering reliable, ethical, and customized recruitment solutions across diverse industries.

The firm is founded and led by Mr. Rajesh Kumar Sharma, a seasoned HR professional with over 30 years of rich industry experience. His deep understanding of organizational needs, manpower planning, and talent acquisition enables us to deliver high-quality hiring outcomes.

  • Strong industry exposure across technical and non-technical roles
  • Focus on long-term partnerships, not just short-term hiring
